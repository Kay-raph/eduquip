About Prime Eduquip Consultants

At Prime Equip Consultants, we believe that education is the key to unlocking a world of opportunities. (core value) We are committed to providing our students with the tools and resources they need to succeed, and to ensuring that they are able to make the most of their time studying abroad. (what we pursue) We facilitate the admission and visa processing by providing educational counselling and support for aspiring students from all over Africa. (present) With a deep understanding of the global education landscape, we empower students to achieve their dreams and make a positive impact on their lives and communities. Through our unwavering dedication to excellence, we are confident that we can help our students achieve their dreams and make a lasting impact on the world.

Mission & Vission

Our vision at Prime Eduquip Consultants is to become the leading Overseas Education Consulting Organization for overseas education. We are dedicated to achieving this goal by providing customised solutions to students who aspire to study abroad. Through our commitment to excellence, we aim to create success stories for all of our students, helping them to achieve their academic and professional goals. In addition, we also strive to add value to our partner institutions. We recognize that our success is inextricably linked to the success of our partners, and as such, we are committed to forging strong, mutually beneficial relationships with institutions across the globe.

Our Core Values

● Commitment
At our company we hold ourselves to a high standard of commitment, both to our clients and to each other. We go above and beyond to keep our promises, always striving to exceed expectations and deliver exceptional service.
● Value Addition
We are dedicated to adding value to society by continuously providing our services and innovative solutions to individual clients in the field of education. Our team goes an extra mile to ensure that our clients are facilitated exceptionally.
● Empathy
We prioritise empathy in all our interactions, understanding that each client has unique needs and circumstances. We strive to build meaningful relationships with our clients, listening carefully to their concerns and offering compassionate and personalised assistance.
● Transparency
We value transparency and believe that honesty is the best policy. We are committed to providing clear and accurate information to our clients, keeping them informed about the services we offer, the costs involved, and any potential risks or limitations.

Our Objectives

Our organisation is dedicated to achieving our mission and vision through a set of specific objectives, which include:
● Developing and enhancing the knowledge and skills of our team members to provide high-quality services to our students.
● Exceeding the established standards set by internationally recognised regulatory and certifying agencies to become qualified to offer recruitment services.
● Obtaining official representation status from globally renowned institutions.
● Actively participating in the marketing plans of leading foreign institutions by organising events, providing infrastructure and administrative facilities.>br> ● Offering language proficiency coaching to students to enhance their prospects of pursuing higher education abroad.
● Preparing students to achieve optimal results in language, aptitude and competitive entrance tests.
● Counselling and advising students on selecting the most appropriate programs and institutions that align with their academic qualifications, while assisting them with admission formalities.
● Providing comprehensive solutions that address all aspects of programme and institution search, applications, scholarships and funding, visa assistance, pre departure preparations, accommodations and post-landing services under the same roof. In addition, we offer language proficiency improvement assistance, ensuring a holistic approach to supporting our clients throughout their academic journey.
● Maintaining a relationship with the family post-recruitment and collecting feedback from students to benefit future students.
● Providing a healthy workplace environment for our employees which serves as a cornerstone in our mission to deliver optimal assistance to our valued clients.

Our Popular Program

Are you interested in studying a degree program in the UK, US or Australia? We have hundreds of programs available, from undergraduate and graduate academic preparation programs to face-to-face and online English language courses, all specially tailored for international students. Explore the options below to find out which INTO program is most suitable for you.


Study English

Across all centers, we offer a variety of English language courses designed to help you develop your language skills for further academic study. These study courses will also prepare you for life in an English-speaking country.

Degree Programs

If you already meet the academic and English language requirements, you can apply for direct entry to a degree program at any of our partner universities in the US and Australia. Degree courses are also available in the UK, at selected universities.

Pathway Graduate Programs

Students who don't meet the requirements for direct entry to a Master's degree, or who want to refresh their language skills and knowledge, can choose from a selection of graduate study abroad programs, either with or without additional English language support, to prepare for a postgraduate degree from a UK or US university.
